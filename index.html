Medical Billing Services

At Providers Care Medical Billing, we handle your end-to-end medical billing needs with accuracy and efficiency. From claim submission to follow-up, we ensure timely reimbursements and minimized claim denials, allowing your practice to focus on patient care while we optimize your revenue cycle.

Virtual assistance

Our Virtual Assistance services streamline administrative tasks like appointment scheduling, insurance verification, billing support, and credentialing follow-ups. With expertise in EHR/EMRs and billing software, we ensure efficiency, accuracy, and compliance, letting you focus on patient care.

Credentialing Services

We help healthcare providers get credentialed and enrolled with insurance networks efficiently. Our team handles the paperwork, tracks application statuses, and ensures your practice is fully credentialed, allowing you to serve more patients and expand your practice.

Accounting & Bookkeeping

We provide accurate and efficient accounting & bookkeeping services, including QuickBooks invoicing, payroll management, account reconciliation, and financial reporting. Our team ensures smooth financial operations, helping healthcare providers maintain compliance and maximize revenue.

Project Management

Customer Service & Tech Support
